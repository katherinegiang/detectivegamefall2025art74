A kitty!


As you turn away from the staring baby, a pitiful meow alerts you to shaking leaves above your head. You spot an orange cat hanging on to a steadily creaking branch; it's about to fall! Quickly, you run under the tree, catching the cat just in time. It meows in your arms, and you decide to wrap it up in your jacket.

The sun is quickly setting, and you don't want to get lost in the forest without light. You make your way back to the rangers' site.
